Getting Started

Set up Tabby Sidecar

Tabby Sidecar is the queue in your pocket — the same account, focus, and clock as the extension, reachable from any phone browser.

📍 Any phone browser — tabatha.pondocean.co/sidecar
1

Open it

Visit tabatha.pondocean.co/sidecar on your phone.

2

Sign in

Tap Continue with Google, or enter your email and use Send magic link — the app shows "✉️ Check your email — tap the link to sign in" while you wait.

3

Install it like an app (optional)

Settings → Account → 📲 Install Tabby adds it to your home screen on Android/desktop Chrome. On iOS: Share → Add to Home Screen.

4

Set a focus

Type into What are you focusing on?, or tap the mic to speak it, then ▶ Set focus.

5

Explore the tabs

Focus (your active intent + queue), Tasks (Asana-synced), Clock (your shift) — plus Settings for everything customizable.
