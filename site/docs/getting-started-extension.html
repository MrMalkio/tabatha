Getting Started

Set up the Browser Extension

The extension is the core of Tabatha: it gates every undirected new tab behind a quick "why are you here?" question, keeps a status bar on every page, and runs the clock. Install it once and it runs quietly from then on.

📍 Chrome (or any Chromium browser) — the core product
1

Load the extension

Load it unpacked at chrome://extensions (or install from the Chrome Web Store once it's published). It adds an always-on status bar — the InBar — to every page, and replaces new-tab with the Tabatha dashboard.

2

Answer the Gatekeeper

The first time you open an undirected new tab, an overlay asks "Why are you here?" Type into What are you working on? and press Continue for a real focus, or pick ⚔️ Side Quest, 🍬 Sugar Box, 🅿️ Park, or 🔖 Later for anything short of one.

Full walkthrough of what each choice does: Focus & intents.
3

Find the InBar

Every page carries a thin status bar along the top: your focus timer, your clock, and quick 🔥 backburner / ⏸ pause buttons. Collapse it to a small ◉ nub if you don't need it visible.

4

Open the sidebar or dashboard

Both show your current focus, the queue, and quick access to Tasks and Work Shifts. The dashboard replaces new-tab; the sidebar replaces Chrome's own tab strip.

5

Sign in

Settings → ☁️ Sync & Account connects your Tabatha account, so the same focus and queue follow you to Tabby Sidecar, Context View, and the desktop companion.
