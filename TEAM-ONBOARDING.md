# Tabatha — Team Onboarding (Reggie & Po)

Welcome to Tabatha 👋 — it's the tool we use to keep our focus, track where our time
goes, and give our context to the team (and our AI agents) so we can move faster
together. This takes about **5 minutes** to set up. If anything's unclear, send feedback
right from inside Tabatha (there's a button) — it goes straight to our task board.

---

## What it does for you
- **Keeps you focused.** When you open a new tab without a clear path, Tabatha gently
  asks "what are you down for?" — a small bit of friction that stops the doom-scroll.
- **Tracks your time for you.** Clock in/out, and it quietly logs where your time and
  attention go — no separate timer app.
- **Remembers your context.** Pick the work back up where you left it; your history
  follows your account, not your computer.

## What it does for the team
- The owner sees **where the team's time goes** (in aggregate) to understand our capacity
  and plan realistically — not to micromanage. It's about clarity, not surveillance.
- Your context helps our **AI agents jump in** and help get work done faster.

---

## Setup — do this once (~5 min)

**1. Install the desktop companion.**
- You'll get a file: **`Tabatha-Desktop-Companion_0.1.0.msi`** (or the `setup.exe`).
- Double-click it → install → it runs in your system tray (bottom-right clock area).
- On first run it creates a folder with the extension inside and pops up a quick guide.

**2. Load the Tabatha extension into Chrome (the companion guides you):**
- Click **Copy extension-folder path** in the companion guide.
- Click **Open chrome://extensions** (or type `chrome://extensions` in Chrome).
- Turn on **Developer mode** (top-right toggle).
- Click **Load unpacked** → paste the copied path → **Select Folder**.
- The companion's guide flips to **✅ installed** when it connects. Pin Tabatha (puzzle-
  piece icon → pin).

**3. Sign in + join the team.**
- Open a new tab — you'll see the Tabatha home/Gatekeeper.
- **Sign in** with your work Google account (or request a magic-link).
- Your account is already set up under our organization, so you're good — but if you're
  asked for an **invite code**, paste the one the owner sends you (Settings → redeem).
- Check the **sidebar** — a small "Synced … ago" chip should go green. That means your
  data is flowing.

**4. Clock in and go.** Use the clock to start your work session. That's it.

## Day-to-day
- **The Gatekeeper** appears on intentional new tabs: state your intent (e.g. "QA on the
  signup flow"), or use **Side Quest (5m)** for a quick detour, **Park** to save a tab for
  later, or **Sugar Box** to stash a fun link as a reward.
- **Backdate an intent** if you forgot to start it — open the focus, edit "Started at."
- **Clock out** for breaks and at end of day.
- **Updates are automatic** — the companion quietly pulls new versions; no reinstalling.

## Privacy — straight talk
- By default Tabatha tracks **metadata** (which app/site, for how long, your stated
  intent) — **not** keystrokes, not page contents.
- **Screen capture is optional and off unless you turn it on.** If you do turn it on:
  frames are redacted, written to **your own machine**, **never uploaded** (not to us,
  not to your owner, not to any AI), and auto-deleted after 30 days — 90 if recorded
  while clocked in. You can switch it off and delete the files yourself, any time.
- **Nobody can turn capture on for you.** There is no admin override today; the setting
  is read only from your own device. If that ever changes, you'll be told first.
- The owner sees **aggregates** of the team's time/context for planning — never your
  captures. Your data is tied to your account and recoverable if you switch machines.
- Full detail: `PRIVACY.md`.
